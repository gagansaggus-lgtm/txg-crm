Architecture

The platform stack — top to bottom.

The team works in TXG Vector. The AI engine is Claude Code, scheduled to run every 15 minutes via the existing Max plan — no API keys. Supabase is the persistent state both layers share. Delivery flows through inexpensive pipes — Resend, Ayrshare, WhatsApp Business API.

Layer 1 — Humans

The Team Works in TXG Vector

Reviews drafts. Approves outreach. Sends LinkedIn DMs manually. Watches dashboards. Closes deals.

↕ reads, writes, approves ↕
Layer 2 — Application

TXG Vector (Next.js)

10-section workspace: Today · Pipeline · Content · Distribution · Outreach · Growth · Strategy · Operations · Analytics · Settings.

↕ queries, mutations, real-time subscriptions ↕
Layer 3 — Source of Truth

Supabase Postgres

32 marketing tables: leads · sequences · messages · content_pieces · social_posts · ai_jobs · ai_job_results · KPI snapshots · attribution touches.

↕ reads pending jobs, writes results ↕
Layer 4 — AI Engine (no API keys)

Claude Code Agent · Runs Every 15 Minutes

Scheduled via Windows Task Scheduler · authenticated through Claude Max plan · processes the ai_jobs queue · validates leads, scores ICP fit, drafts outreach, generates content, scrapes competitors.

↓ approved by humans, sent via APIs ↓
Layer 5 — Delivery (not AI)

Resend · Ayrshare · WhatsApp Business

Email, social posting (LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, Twitter), WhatsApp broadcast — these are pipes, not AI. Sub-$200/month total.

What we sell

One headline service. Six secondary capabilities.

Marketing leads with fulfillment. Everything else is a capability we offer when the opportunity arrives — never turning down good business, never diluting the lead message either.

▣

E-Commerce Fulfillment for North America

The entire order lifecycle handled — receive inventory, store, pick, pack, ship, manage returns. Native integrations with Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce. Two facilities cover 80% of Canada and 70% of the US. This is the headline our marketing leads with.

Secondary

Warehousing Only

Bulk storage, no fulfillment. Pallet-in, pallet-out. For brands that handle their own pick-pack.

Secondary

Amazon FBA Prep

Labeling, kitting, bundling, polybagging — all FBA-compliant — then shipped to Amazon fulfillment centers.

Secondary

Last-Mile Delivery

Local last-mile in NA where our fleet covers. Branded experience, faster delivery, full tracking.

Secondary

Returns Management

Reverse logistics: receive, inspect, restock, refurb, refund. Critical for international brands.

Secondary

Cross-Border (India → NA)

India D2C brands shipping to NA — direct courier, hybrid, or full local fulfillment models.

Secondary

Custom 4PL

Full supply-chain orchestration when a brand wants us to manage everything end-to-end.
